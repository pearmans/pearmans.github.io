Hi, I'm Stuart.


Ever since I was a kid I've always loved building things. My craft has matured since then, but I still haven't lost the joy I get from making new creations. I specialize in front-end web development, but I am always trying to expand my skillset with fresh projects in all imagineable categories.

Web Work

The day I wrote my first snippet of HTML was the day I started to think about a future in web design. A few weeks later I started to realize that it may not be so far off. Here are a few things I've made in my short time as a web designer.

  • Element 84

    Site redesign and blog migration for Element 84, Inc.

  • Learn With Stu

    A blog made to share my progress while I learned to design for the web.

Odd Projects
